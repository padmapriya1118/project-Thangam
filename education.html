Career

He joined Valar Ads Company (Advertisement Agency) in 1999. In 1993, Thanga Mohideen Pitchai started Valar Ads. In September 2007, Shaik Mydeen Thangam and his brother Abdul Salam Thangam started Thangam Monthly Magazine. Shaik Mydeen is the publisher and editor of Thangam Magazine. The Thangam Brothers started Thangam Group, where Shaik Mydeen is the Founder Chairman and Managing Director.
